Questions? We got you.

Is there a contract or commitment?

Nope. Both plans are month-to-month. Stay because it's working, not because you're locked in. We recommend giving SEO at least 3 months to see real traction, but you can cancel anytime.

What's the difference between the one-time fix and a monthly plan?

The one-time fix is a deep audit and repair of your existing site — schema, meta tags, structured data, AI signals. It's a snapshot fix. Monthly plans include that ongoing maintenance plus content creation, city pages, backlinks, and strategic growth. Think of the fix as a tune-up; monthly plans are the full pit crew.

Do you work with platforms other than Spectora?

Yes. We work with Spectora, HomeGauge, InspectorPages, WordPress, Squarespace, and custom sites. If your site runs on the internet, we can optimize it.

How fast will I see results?

Technical fixes (schema, meta, structured data) can impact rankings within 2-4 weeks. Content-driven SEO (city pages, blogs, backlinks) typically shows meaningful movement in 60-90 days. We'll set honest expectations upfront — no "page 1 in 7 days" nonsense.

I already pay someone for SEO. Why switch?

Ask them to show you exactly what they've done in the last 3 months. If they can't point to specific pages built, schema added, or rankings moved — you're paying for reports, not results. We'll give you a free audit so you can compare.

Do you only work with home inspectors?

We started with home inspectors because it's what we know best. But our SEO approach works for any trades business — electricians, plumbers, HVAC, roofers. If you serve local customers and need to be found online, we can help.

Can I pay with crypto?

Yes! We accept stablecoins (USDC) through Stripe at checkout, plus all major cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. More crypto options coming soon.
